Step 02 · On-Platform Matching
Intelligent Buyer Matching — the same engine, for human buyers on clutch.co
Why it matters: the MCP layer serves external agents. This is the human-facing equivalent — buyers describe their project in plain English instead of clicking filters, and the same AI that serves Claude and ChatGPT returns ranked providers with clear reasoning. It gets smarter over time by tracking which matches lead to closed projects. Scroll up to the hero or click the floating "I need a recommendation…" pill to try it live.

Natural Language Matching · Interactive Demo

Live
Try it now — 2 ways
① Click a popular search chip at the top of the page → hit Get Matched.
② Click the "I need a recommendation…" pill in the bottom-right → talk to the agent.
What makes this different from filters
Instead of clicking 8 dropdowns, buyers describe what they need. The model reasons across provider profiles, reviews, past projects, pricing, and outcomes — and returns matches with plain-english reasoning for each pick. That's the same output the MCP layer gives to AI agents.
